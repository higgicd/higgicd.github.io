# Research Projects

## Ongoing Projects

I am always looking for exceptional candidates to join my research team as Research Assistants or postgraduate research students. Those with a background in GIS with knowledge of spatial econometrics and statistics and/or capturing and modelling human mobility patterns are particularly encouraged to apply.

## Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme

Outstanding students are encouraged to apply to the University Grants Council’s Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme. This prestigious fellowship offers a generous funding package to support your PhD studies. Applications to the scheme are due December 1 of each year. Highly-ranked candidates that do not receive the fellowship are eligible for internal PolyU Postgraduate Studentships. See our [PolyU](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/ro/hkphd-fellowship/index.html) and [Faculty](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/fce/study-at-fce/research-postgraduate-programmes) sites for more information.
